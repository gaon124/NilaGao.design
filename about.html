About

I'm a product designer at Klaviyo, designing personalization and AI-driven experiences for marketing technology. My work focuses on making complex systems intuitive, trustworthy, and delightful.

I hold a Master's degree in Product Development from Carnegie Mellon University. Raised in New York, I grew up in my family's Chinese restaurants, where observing menus, packaging, and customer interactions sparked my interest in user experience and product behavior.

I design with emotion at the core—believing great products create memorable moments, like tearing open a Chinese takeout bag and catching the first smell of crispy General Tso's chicken. I aim to craft experiences that feel human, engaging, and lasting.

Outside of design, I enjoy running, dancing, and hiking.
